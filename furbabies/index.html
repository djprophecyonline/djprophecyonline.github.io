About Us

Welcome to
Fur Babies

Established in 2019, Fur Babies offers a cost effective solution to ensure the love and care of your Fur Family, as well as your peace of mind, while you travel. With many references and strong connections with the Community Policing Forums and WhatsApp Community Groups, Fur Babies has established a reputation of honesty, integrity and reliability throughout KZN. Now there is no need to send your pets to kennels and catteries when they can remain in their own environment and continue with their regular routines while they wait for your return. The focus of Fur Babies is a relationship built on trust, which is why we send photos and updates to our clients each day, and we always keep our lines of communication open as far as is possible. Come and join the Fur Babies Family. Feel free to contact us regarding bookings, pricing, reviews or any other questions you may have. We look forward to meeting you and your loved ones.

Our Services

Explore Our Services

House Sitting

Your home and belongings are important which is why professional conduct, integrity and trust are the core of Fur Babies. Our references testify to the manner in which we maintain cleanliness, operate within defined boundaries and respect the privacy of our clients.
Pet Care

As the primary focus of our company, the animals we bond with are our most treasured joy. Whether it be going for a walk, taking a bath or enjoying play time, every Fur Baby is loved, cared for and comforted while you travel.
Plant Care

Certain plants and flowers need constant nurturing and this is where our green fingers come in to maintain the life of your flora. From watering and feeding to providing sunlight, we understand the value of time invested in growing and grooming our Green Babies.
Security

The security of your possessions is a key part of our policy which is why we are connected to all operational Community Policing Forums in the areas of our services. We are also joined to the various Facebook and WhatsApp Community Groups.
Special Needs

For those of our Fur Babies which have disabilities or require special medical attention, we are fully able to follow any guidelines which you provide for us to ensure the comfort of your loved ones while you travel.
